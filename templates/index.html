RSA Encryption

We don't use standard libraries blindly. We implement the RSA algorithm mathematically to generate unique public/private key pairs for every user.

How it Works

When you register, the system calculates two large prime numbers to generate your unique Public Key (shared with friends) and Private Key (kept secret on your device).

Before chatting, the system performs a cryptographic handshake to ensure the identity of both parties.
